Regression for Age at tubal ligation

For the middle portion of the table, you would need to group age at TL (column 57) as 

Group 1: 22-30, Group 2: 31-35, Group 3: 36-40, Group 4: 41-56, Group 0: Never had TL (reference).  

This categorical predictor, along with the previously created grouped-age predictor, would be needed in a logistic regression model to get the OR for each age at TL group relative to the refence (Never had TL).  The authors then either used column 57 directly, or treated the grouped age at TL variable, as a quantitative predictor for the trend test.  I am not sure whether the authors considered grouped-age (computed from column 3) as a quantitative or categorical predictor in the logistic regression analyses.
